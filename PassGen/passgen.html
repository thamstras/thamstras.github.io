PassGen, a (probably) cryptographically secure password generator.

Accepts as command line arguments -c, -l and -m.

-c is how many passwords to generate, -l is the length of passwords to generate and -m is which character sets to use. 1 = upper/lower case letters, 2 = numbers, 4 = special characters.

Recommended modes are 3 (alphanumeric) or 7 (all allowed characters)

For reasons which I hope are obvious, no pre-compiled binary is available.
